# Sign-up

To get started, follow the steps below:

1. Sign up for Widgetbook Cloud by visiting [widgetbook.io](https://www.widgetbook.io/plans) and selecting an appropriate plan.
2. Check your email for further instructions and follow the provided steps.

**New to Widgetbook Cloud?**

- Create a new account by choosing the Sign-up option and providing the necessary information.
- Once the sign-up process is complete, you will be automatically logged in.

**Existing Widgetbook Cloud user?**

- Sign in using your existing login credentials.

**Forgot your password?**

- Utilize the password recovery feature to reset your password.
- Please note that the password recovery link will expire after a few hours for security purposes.
